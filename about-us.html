SPRINT // Motorsports: British Motorsports Excellence

SPRINT // Motorsports is a British motorsport engineering company built on precision, craftsmanship, and performance-led design. Rooted in the UK’s rich motorsport heritage, we specialise in creating one-off composite body kits and components for high-performance applications, combining advanced engineering with hands-on fabrication expertise.

We believe true performance comes from balance, engineering accuracy, material integrity and design harmony working as one to create components without concession.

Through bespoke aerodynamic and structural solutions, we transform ideas into track-ready reality. Whether developing a single prototype or a complete build, we deliver components that reflect our commitment to performance, individuality, and uncompromising standards.

Design-Led Thinking

Every project begins with intent. We approach design as a problem-solving discipline, where performance targets, material behaviour, and real-world use shape every decision from the outset.

Crafted in Composites

Our work is rooted in hands-on composite craftsmanship. With deep experience across carbon, aramid, and glass, we combine traditional techniques with modern processes to deliver parts built with purpose and precision.

Performance Focused

Motorsport is our reference point. From aerodynamics to structural integrity, we develop solutions guided by function, efficiency, and the pursuit of measurable performance.

Meet The One Man Team

Not because the project was small, but because it wasn’t.

From concept through build, every decision stayed with the same person. There were no hand-offs, no reinterpretations and definitely no moments where responsibility dissolved into a department. When something needed changing: it was changed by the person who designed it - not explained to someone else.

What stood out wasn’t speed or scale, but correctness. The work was taken on selectively, scoped realistically and executed end-to-end by someone who clearly stands behind every line drawn and every choice made.

For complex, high-consequence work, that level of ownership mattered more than the size of the team.

Oscar EJ Rawles

Track-Bred Tradition

Motorsport thinking defines everything we do. Shaped by Britain’s deep racing heritage, our work blends competition-driven engineering with meticulous, hands-on craftsmanship.

As a company, we focus on precision-led design, in-house development, and advanced composite construction. From initial concept to final manufacture, every project is guided by performance intent, material expertise, and disciplined attention to detail.

This is British motorsport engineering with purpose - where experience, innovation, and passion converge to create components that stand apart. It’s not just about building parts; it’s about building meaning into every detail.
